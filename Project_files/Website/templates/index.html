Smart Pothole Detection System

Smart Pothole Detection System is an innovative technology aimed at identifying and reporting potholes on roads in real-time and providing a map that can be used to avoid said potholes. By using Artificial Intelligence and the phone's resources and sensors, this technology ensures that maintenance teams can address road issues promptly, enhancing road safety and reducing accidents and vehicle damage.
